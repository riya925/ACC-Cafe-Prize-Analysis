    Gyro Platter

    Enjoy a taste of Greece with our Gyro Platter. It's a satisfying meal with sliced flavorful meat, fresh veggies, and warm pita bread. Whether you're hungry for lunch or dinner, this platter is a great choice.

    The meat in our Gyro Platter is soaked in delicious flavors that remind you of Greek food. Each piece is really flavorful!

    Crunchy veggies:
    We add crisp and fresh vegetables to the platter. They go so well with the meat and add a nice crunch to every bite.

    Soft pita bread:
    The platter also comes with soft pita bread that's warm and cozy. You can use it to wrap up the meat and veggies or to mop up hummus.

    When to enjoy:
    If you're looking for a quick and tasty meal, our Gyro Platter is perfect. It's great for lunch or dinner when you want something good to eat without any fuss.

    A Message From the CEO

    Back in 2012, I had a dream to bring together two of my favorite foods from around the world. Ever since that vision, i acquired a highly dedicated team of researchers and professionals to create a concept that the community would love best. After years of production and hard work, Döner & Gyros was born and made its debut in 2014.
