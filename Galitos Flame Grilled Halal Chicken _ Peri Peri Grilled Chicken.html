peri peri perfection

Whatever your occasion, we make it one to remember!

Galito's halal flame grilled Piri Piri chicken is just the right kind of comfort food. It's a bit spicy and has LOTS of tasty flavors. Our traditional marinade is made from all-natural ingredients infused with select herbs and spices. Slowly marinated for at least 24 hours, flame grilled to perfection, and served to order, making it the healthiest, tastiest flame grilled chicken in town.
