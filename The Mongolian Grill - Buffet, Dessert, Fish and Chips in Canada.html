OUR SPECIAL

the grill

The grill is our one-of-a-kind over-sized wok. It is one seamless piece of forged steel. Heated by natural gas to a searing 700 degrees. This huge grill can handle over a dozen meals simul-taneously. In traditional Mongolian style, the wok is tended to using swords. We have continued that tradition with our con-cept. Watch our grill masters stir-fry your creation right in front of you using our custom made blades.
